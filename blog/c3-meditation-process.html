How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I need to be constantly reminding myself that I should be building on my process skills not focusing on how something looks. But that part of my lizard brain that says "No one's going to buy this if it doesn't look amazing. What are you going to do when you're homeless? Survival of the fittest! ROOAAAW HISSS HISS!" Yeah, so that kicks in and I end up battling between wanting to take shortcuts that I know will (half arsed) work but that totally go against proper clean process. At some point I'm hoping to acheive both.
