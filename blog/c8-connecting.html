Connect the dots

Have you tried using any of the insight and techniques for growing trust and sincerity? How did it go?

Yes but not consciously on the most part. But I have been in a few situations where the '5 Dysfunctions of a Team' were fiercely present. If there is one thing that those experiences taught me it's that without Trust (in which I would include the benefit of the doubt) everything goes to hell. I could have really used those techniques then.

Try empathic listening - how did it go?

Emphatic listening 'Just Like Me' exercises are something that I find waaay too easy to do. I slip easily into identifying with whomever I'm talking with without realizing. But the judgey psycho analysis listening is something I've been subjected to a lot! I think those experiences of people going all Freudian on you is really common pattern for people to slip into without realizing and it's the main reason that I lean more heavily towards the emphatic way of listening because I've had so many people do that to me and it kinda sucks.

How did you find the loving kindness meditation?

I've tried this before. I was taught this technique a few years ago as a form of forgiveness. If I'm doing a "Think well of everybody" kinda buzz I'm fine. Feeling happy. Love and peace to all. If I have to do this exercise with specific people in mind I will start off ok. Yeah, first person cool. Second person OK. Then slowly I go down this dark path where I'm bring up past hurts, failings, betrayals - nek minit, I've gone full blown Darth Vader. Yeah... So I don't tend to do that exercise cause I get all "Join the dark side!".

Context

This is a very regular legit polar point of view occurrence at my place between my partner Francis and I. Like for real this happens ALL the time. Francis wants a cookie or whatever. By 'A' cookie he means 99% of the packet. He wants to eat all the cookies. I want to save some for another day. And I can't eat ALL the cookies in a single sitting let alone in a 5 min window. Spoiler Alert: All the cookies were eaten.

Pearl's Point of View

I want to have one or two cookies and save the rest for another day. I don't think we need to eat all the cookies right now. The cookies are quite sweet and can make you feel sick if you eat too many.

Francis' Point of View

I'm just going to eat one more. We have cookies and they should be eaten - we don't need to ration them out over the week. I mean, they're here, what are we waiting for? We should eat them. But they are just SITTING THERE! I need a COOKIE! ARRRRHHHH!!!
